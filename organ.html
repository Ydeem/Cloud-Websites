FAQ

Do I need a piano at home?

Any 88‑key digital piano with weighted keys or an acoustic piano works. We can recommend budget‑friendly options.

Are lessons available online?

Yes. We use a high‑quality audio setup and multi‑camera angles for clarity. You’ll receive lesson notes after each session.

Can I prepare for exams?

Absolutely—ABRSM and Trinity syllabi supported. We’ll map your timeline and run mock exams.

What’s your cancellation policy?

Reschedule up to 24 hours before your lesson time. Missed lessons without notice are charged.
